FireOwl Studios (Previously known as: Gear Studios) is a game studio founded in 2018 by: Livin' On The Streetz (streetz) and Hewres. The studio began with making games on roblox.com. The studio strives to make original and fun games for everyone to enjoy, both 3D and 2D. The current team consists of 3 people: streetz, Hewres and PeterPLJR.
